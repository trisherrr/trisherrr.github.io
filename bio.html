bio

Trisha Bantigue currently resides in San Francisco Bay Area as a UC Berkeley student, Public Speaking and Debate teacher, and pursues pageantry and modeling on the side. She is currently 21 years old and her ethnicity is Filipino. She was born in Pampanga, Philippines and came to Las Vegas, NV when she was 10 years old. Then, at 16 she started modeling in runways represented by her first agency, Press Talent. She started doing photoshoots and later started booking web and print ad campaigns. She has experience with acting within commercial jobs but her main expertise is fashion, editorial, and commercial prints. She also holds various pageant titles such as Miss National Asia 2014, Miss Philippines USA 2nd Runner Up, Miss Santa Clara (Miss America Org) 2nd Runner Up, and now, currently Miss California Earth 2016.
